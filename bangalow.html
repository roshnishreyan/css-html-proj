McCluskieganj is one of the foremost tourist attractions of Jharkhand. Many tourists from the nearby states of Bihar and West Bengal visit this scenic town to spend some “quiet time”. Ideal as a weekend destination Hill Side Bungalow located in Mccluskieganj,Jharkhand was built in the 1860’s is a heritage bungalow. The rooms have magnificient view and an old world Charm. You could hear the birds chirping, mooing of the distant cow, roar of a car engine for a few seconds, whiffs of fresh air, gentle sunlight kissing the body and the vacant silence of the valley. Such that nature and wilderness are merged the natural way.
